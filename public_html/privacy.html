Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Welcome to ScottishInsults.com — where the barbs are sharp and your privacy is respected.

What We Know About You

Honestly? Not much. We don't collect your name, email, or any personal secrets. But like most websites, we do work with a couple of third-party services that do gather some basic info:

  • Google Analytics tells us how people are using the site — like what pages you visit, how long you stay, and where you came from.
  • Google AdSense helps us show you ads — sometimes based on your browsing habits.

What Kind of Info Are We Talking About?

The usual anonymous stuff:

  • Your IP address
  • Your browser type
  • What device you're using
  • How long you loitered on our site
  • What pages you clicked on before getting distracted by cat videos

This data helps us figure out what's working, what's not, and occasionally pays the hosting bills with ads that aren't completely irrelevant.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your data.
  • We don't know your name.
  • We don't send emails (because we don't even collect them).
  • We don't stalk you. That's creepy.

Who's Actually Watching?

Our partners in data crime (not actual crime, just standard web stuff):

  • Google Analytics – for usage stats (privacy policy)
  • Google AdSense – for ads (privacy policy)

These folks might use cookies or other tracking tools. You can read their policies to get the fine print.

You're in Control

Here's how you can take back some power:

  • Opt out of Google Analytics
  • Tweak Google Ads settings
  • Clear your cookies like it's spring cleaning
